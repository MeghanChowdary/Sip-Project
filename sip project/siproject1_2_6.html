STOMACHE ACHE
REMEDIES TO REDUCE STOMACH ACHE:-
•Lemon and Honey
•Pomegranate Seeds
•Mint Oil
•Home recipe for acid reflux
Procedure for the recipe:-
1.Lemon and Honey
Take 1 glass of warm water and add 1 teaspoon of lemon juice and 1 teaspoon of honey. Drink this mixture after every meal. This is one of the best and most effective home remedies for indigestion.
2.Pomegranate Seeds
Pomegranate when mixed with black salt and pepper is a great remedy for stomach pains.
3.Mint Oil
Add 2 drops of mint oil in a cup of warm water and drink immediately. It provides instant relief from gripping and flatulence.
4.Home recipe for acid reflux
Yogurt, water, rock salt, roasted jeera, ginger and coriander leaves.
Combine 1/4 cup plain yogurt with 3/4 cup water (or double this, keeping same ratio).
Mix well.
Add 1 tsp rock salt, pinch of roasted jeera (cumin) powder, a bit of grated ginger, and fresh coriander leaves.
